Start Up Funding

Funding for the planning and implementation (1st year) of Helim Changemaker Project will be supported by three different types of sources.

HUMANITARIAN INNOVATION FUND

  • A supporter of humanitarian organizations that seeks to identify, nurture, and share innovative and scalable solutions to today's challenges to humanity and humanitarian assistance.
  • Up to $33,000 for the planning phase.
  • Up to $250,000 for the implementation phase.

PEANUT BUTTER PROJECT

  • A major RUTF manufacturer and distributer in the fight against child hunger and malnutrition.
  • PBP are experts in the planning and implementation of factories for local production of RUTFs, as well as in the acquisition of funding to do so.
  • PBP will cover the cost of the factory that Helim Changemaker Project is unable to do, and will receive profit proportionately.

DONORS

  • Through a dynamic fundraising strategy, Helim Changemaker project fundraiser will reach a wide array of donors
  • A transparent, appealing and convincing social media identity will expand the reach of the project to the whole World Wide Web, making it easy to get involved or donate.
  • Through in-person meeting and events, the project will reach large and small donors alike.
