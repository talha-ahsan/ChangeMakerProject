Locally Available Raw materials
  • Local production of peanuts: 155 MT of peanuts in 2014. Major growth in production in 2007, stable since 2009. 96.8% of total peanut production is committed for domestic consumption.

  • Local production of palm oil: 60 MT in 2014. Stable production since 2013.
  • Domestic availability of high quality dry skimmed milk: Moisture, fat, protein, ash, and lactose of locally packed milk powders are almost insignificantly different (p<0.05) and they are comparable to the compositional quality in US and Argentina.
