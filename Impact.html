Impact

~30 new jobs per year in the factory, including production, administration and support staff.

~312 new trained personnel per year graduating with a course from ARC’s Academy

~ 60% of positions at the factory are staffed by women, from the production line to administration.

~230 women graduating every year with at least one course from ARC’s academy

~1200 MT of RUTFs per year, enough to get 82,760 children through severe acute malnutrition.

~90 new Nutrition-trained community workers graduating each year from the ARC’s academy.
