Implementation Strategy

RUTFs Factory

  1. Identify a willing partner with proven effectiveness in the local production of RUTFs.
  2. Agree on a joint project for establishing a factory in Darfur: timeline, budget, financing strategy, purchasers, management, monitoring, and exit strategy.
  3. Scan the field for availability and quality of raw material supplies, strategic location, recruitment staff, and other necessary assessments.
  4. Construct the factory and recruit factory staff.
  5. Begin production; first batches of RUTF sold.
  6. First cycles of income for workers and of financial support for the ARC Academy.

ARC's E-Learning Academy

  1. In partnership with the women of Kalma camp, community members, partners, donors and others, identify the main types of courses to be offered.
  2. Identify existing and missing technological and human resources necessary for the establishment of the Academy.
  3. Through initial donor sponsorship and grants, secure funding to purchase essential materials and hire personnel to run the Academy.
  4. With experts and a volunteer Arabic translator, develop the virtual courses and modules from HQ.
  5. Set up location, recruit staff and build management structure for the opening and running of the Academy. Cover maintenance, management, and security needs.
